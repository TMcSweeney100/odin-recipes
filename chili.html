Chili

The great thing about chili is that in less then 30 minutes your done, and its really easy to reheat for another meal

What you need.
  • 1 pound of Ground steak or any ground meat of your choice
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika, cumin and chili powder
  1. Cook the ground mince with the onion
  2. Once the mince is browned add in the rest of the ingredients
  3. Bring to the boil and then turn the heat and simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Serve with Rice or tortillas
